Avoid duplication

## Completed phases:
- Summary → `planning/COMPLETED_PHASES.md`
- Delete `planning/phase-X_*.md`
- `SCORING_IMPROVEMENT_PLAN.md`: status + oneline summary + "See `planning/COMPLETED_PHASES.md`"

## Ongoing phases:
- Details → `planning/phase-X_*.md`
- `SCORING_IMPROVEMENT_PLAN.md`: mirror + oneline summary + "See `planning/phase-X_*.md`" + depends on / unlocks phase x, y, z
